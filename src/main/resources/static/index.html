Questions Regarding Packages



  • Destination Selection:Tour packages offer a wide range of destinations to choose from, catering to various interests and preferences. These can include popular tourist spots, historical landmarks, natural wonders, cultural experiences, or adventure-filled locations.
  • Accommodation and Transportation: Tour packages often include arrangements for accommodation and transportation.
  • Guided Tours: Many tour packages provide professional guides who accompany travelers throughout their journey. These guides offer valuable insights, historical context, and local knowledge about the destinations, enhancing the overall travel experience.
  • Group or Customized Options:Some packages can customize on personal preference
  • Convenience and Peace of Mind:One of the significant advantages of tour packages is the convenience they offer. They take care of logistical details, allowing travelers to relax and enjoy their vacation without worrying about planning or organizing. Additionally, tour operators often provide customer support and assistance throughout the trip.
  • Cost and Value: Tour packages often provide cost savings compared to planning and booking everything individually. The package price typically covers most of the essentials like accommodation, transportation, and guided tours. Travelers can have a better idea of the total cost upfront, avoiding unexpected expenses.
  • Safety and Security:Reputed tour operators prioritize the safety and security of their travelers. They have established networks, contacts, and local knowledge to ensure a secure and hassle-free experience. This includes selecting reliable accommodation options, arranging safe transportation, and providing guidance on any potential risks.

Questions regarding how to book a package

  • Research and gather information:Start by conducting thorough research on the package you want to book. Collect details about the destination, accommodations, activities, and any other relevant information.
  • Travel Dates:Determine the dates for your trip. If possible, try to be flexible as it can help you find better deals and availability.
  • Package Type: Decide on the type of package you want to book. Packages can include accommodation, transportation, activities, and meals. Consider whether you prefer an all-inclusive package or if you want to customize certain aspects of your trip.
  • Contact Travel Agencies: Reach out to travel agencies or tour operators online, by phone, or in person to inquire about the package you are interested in. Ask for detailed information, including inclusions, exclusions, payment terms, and cancellation policies.
  • Read the Terms and Conditions:Carefully review the terms and conditions before confirming your booking. Pay attention to cancellation policies, refund policies, travel insurance, and any additional fees or charges.
  • Stay Organized:Keep all your booking details, confirmations, and receipts in a safe place. Make copies or save digital versions as backup. This will help you stay organized and easily access important information during your trip.
  • Payment: Once you have finalized the package, make the necessary payment according to the travel agency's instructions. Ensure that you receive a confirmation of your booking, along with any relevant vouchers or tickets.

Legal requirements for overseas tourists

  • Valid Passport: Overseas tourists visiting Sri Lanka must possess a valid passport with a minimum validity of six months from the date of entry into the country.
  • ETA (Electronic Travel Authorization): The ETA is an online visa approval system that allows tourists to obtain a visa for short visits to Sri Lanka. It must be obtained before arrival and is typically valid for 30 days. Extensions may be possible.
  • Vaccinations: Vaccinations: Some vaccinations may be required before traveling to Sri Lanka, depending on the visitor's country of origin. It is recommended to check with the Sri Lankan embassy or consulate in your home country for specific requirements.
  • Travel Insurance: While not mandatory, having travel insurance that covers medical expenses and emergencies is highly recommended.
  • Respect for Local Laws and Customs:Tourists are expected to abide by Sri Lankan laws and respect local customs, traditions, and religious practices.
  • Proof of Funds:Tourists may be required to show proof of sufficient funds to cover their expenses during their stay in Sri Lanka. This could include bank statements, credit card statements, or cash.
  • Return or Onward Ticket:Visitors are usually required to have a return or onward ticket, demonstrating their intention to leave the country at the end of their authorized stay.
